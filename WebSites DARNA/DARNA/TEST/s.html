Welcome to DARNA !

"Darna” is a mobile app connecting homeowners with skilled artisans for affordable and punctual home services. Users can view client reviews, and artisans can easily manage their schedules and accept or decline requests. Simplifying the process, Darna aims to streamline home service solutions for both parties.

Awsome Features

Darna offers its users the best services through its awesome features . Explore it !!

Client

initiate service requests and review the profiles and feedback of artisans before confirming or declining their offers , access to lists of scheduled services .

Admin

manage users and the various services offered within the application, including creating artisan accounts and handling reports regarding artisans.

Artisan

accepting client requests, scheduling appointments, and seamless communication via the integrated messaging system with clients.

Comes With All You Need

DARNA app connects clients and artisans for home services. Clients can find local artisans, request services, and manage their accounts. Artisans can showcase their skills and find work opportunities.
